404 Not Found Error

By:Eriberto Ramos-Lara

MY POEM

404 Not Found Error in my code.
Filled with "heads", "titles", and "headers" galore.
I can't seem to find the 404 Not found Error in my code.
It might be that I forgot to add an end close bracket.
Or maybe its because I spilled a hot sauce packet.
404 Not Found in my code.
I think I need to troubleshoot
And hop the hoop in this code.
Or maybe I'll just jump ship.
And give up on this 404 Not Found Error.
